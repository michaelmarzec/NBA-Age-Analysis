Glossary

  • Average Age: Mean age per team
  • Average Age (by minutes): Mean age weighted by minutes played per team
  • Average Age (by usage): Mean age weighted by usage rate per team
  • Point: Mean age of 'Point' position weighted by minutes played per team
  • Combo: Mean age of 'Combo' position weighted by minutes played per team
  • Wing: Mean age of 'Wing' position weighted by minutes played per team
  • Forward: Mean age of 'Forward' position weighted by minutes played per team
  • Big: Mean age of 'Big' position weighted by minutes played per team
  • *Excludes players who have played in less than 70% of team games played
